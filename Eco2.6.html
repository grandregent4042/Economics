The Core Connecting Story
RBI = India's Central Bank (est. 1935, nationalised 1949) → Controls money supply via monetary policy → Regulates ALL banks, NBFCs, financial institutions → Manages forex reserves (Safety · Liquidity · Return) under FEMA 1999 → Acts as Banker to Govt (WMA, G-sec issuance) → Issues currency except ₹1 note → DICGC insures deposits up to ₹5 lakh per bank → Oversees all payment systems via NPCI (UPI, IMPS, RuPay) → Maintains financial stability as Lender of Last Resort.
