The Core Connecting Idea
Fiscal Policy = Government's lever of Tax + Spending to steer the economy. Paired with Monetary Policy (RBI), it's the complete toolkit. The art lies in knowing when to expand and when to consolidate — always working against the business cycle, never with it.
📈 Expansionary = Recession Fix 📉 Contractionary = Inflation Fix ⚖️ Counter-Cyclical = Good ⚡ Pro-Cyclical = Dangerous 📊 Consolidation = Discipline
