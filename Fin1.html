The Core Connecting Story
Money Market = RBI's Liquidity Control Tool — Short-term (under 1 year), manages day-to-day money supply in the economy.
Capital Market = Long-term Growth Engine — Channels savings into investment for development. Regulated by SEBI (corporate) and RBI (Govt securities).
Short term = Stability & Liquidity · Long term = Investment & Development
⏱️ <1yr = Money Mkt 📅 ≥1yr = Capital Mkt 🏦 RBI = Govt/Banks 📋 SEBI = Corporates 📉 Rate↑ = Price↓ 🔺 Trilemma = 2 of 3 🥇 Bond → Pref → Equity
