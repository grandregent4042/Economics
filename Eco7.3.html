⑦ E-Commerce & WTO — Critical Issue 🔥
India's Position — Very Important

⚖️ WTO E-Commerce Negotiations

India is NOT part of WTO e-commerce negotiations
WTO talks aim to make permanent the moratorium on customs duties on electronic transmissions. India has temporarily agreed NOT to impose such duties — but risks losing future tariff rights if it becomes permanent.
Current: Temporary Moratorium
India agreed temporarily — not permanently
Risk if Permanent
India loses future tariff rights & revenue
India's Stand
