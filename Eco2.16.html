Current Account + Capital Account = Δ Forex Reserves
THE GOLDEN IDENTITY — ALWAYS HOLDS TRUE

The Connecting Thread
India's external sector flows → captured by BoP → Current A/c (trade, services, remittances) + Capital A/c (FDI, FPI, ECB) → net change in Forex Reserves. Stability of Forex determines India's convertibility decisions. Full current-account convertibility (since 1993) is the foundation. Gradual capital account liberalization + deep markets = path to Rupee internationalization. You cannot have internationalization without tackling capital account convertibility first.
📊 Trade → Current A/c 💼 Investment → Capital A/c 🏦 Net = Forex Change 🔄 Forex Stability → Convertibility 🌐 Convertibility → Internationalization
