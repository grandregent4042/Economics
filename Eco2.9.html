⚠️ Exam Traps — Commonly Wrong

⚠️ Statement Traps

❌ "NBFCs can issue cheques"
✅ Only banks can issue cheques on themselves
❌ "NABARD gives direct loans to farmers"
✅ NABARD provides refinance only — not direct
❌ "Payment Banks can give loans"
✅ Payment Banks CANNOT lend at all
❌ "P2P platforms can raise deposits"
✅ P2P cannot raise deposits or give credit guarantee

The Big Picture — Indian Financial System
Banks (accept deposits, issue cheques) + NBFCs (lend, invest, lease — NO deposits) form the financial system. Banks → Commercial (PSB/Private/Foreign/RRB/LAB) + Cooperative (UCB + 3-tier rural). AIFIs (NABARD, NHB, EXIM, SIDBI) provide long-term sectoral finance via refinance. Niche banks (Payment Banks = no lending, Small Finance Banks = PSL-focused) enhance inclusion. CICs provide credit data; PDs support govt debt market.
🏦 Banks = Deposits ⚠️ NBFCs = No Deposits 🌾 NABARD 1982 📍 RRB 50:15:35 📱 PB = No Lending 75% SFB PSL ⚠️ PACS ≠ RBI
