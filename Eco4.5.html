The Core Connecting Idea
India built a legal framework (FRBM 2003) to prevent inflationary deficit financing. The 1997 reform ended direct money-printing. Now, the challenge is enforcement — FRBM targets are often missed through off-budget routes. A Fiscal Council would add an independent oversight layer to make fiscal discipline real, not just on paper.
