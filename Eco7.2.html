Prelims Traps & Tricks

⚡ Watch Out For

🔴 Export ≠ Turnover for MSME
Export turnover NOT counted in classification limit
🔴 IIP ≠ GDP
IIP = Industrial output only, not overall economic growth
🔴 Core Industries ≠ IIP
Core = 40.27% of IIP, not all of it
🔴 PLI ≠ Subsidy
Incentive given on incremental sales, not upfront
🔴 KVIC ≠ MSME Ministry
KVIC is a statutory body under MSME Ministry
🔴 Refinery = Top Core Industry
28.04% weight — highest among 8 core industries
⚙️
The Core Connecting Idea
MSMEs generate employment but need policy support → Govt schemes (Udyam, RAMP, CHAMPIONS) plug gaps → KVIC supports rural micro-enterprises → IIP measures industrial pulse → Core industries drive heavy output → PLI incentivises domestic manufacturing to cut import dependence.
Together: MSMEs + Policy + IIP measurement + PLI = India's Atmanirbhar Industrial Ecosystem
