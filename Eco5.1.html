The Core Connecting Idea
British distorted land rights → Independent India corrected via Abolition, Tenancy Protection, Ceiling & Consolidation.
Modern reforms focus on Fair Acquisition (LA Act 2013), Legal Leasing (MALLA 2016), Land Pooling & Digitization.
Mughal → State ownership British → Privatised + exploited 1947 → 4 Reforms 2013 → Fair acquisition Modern → Digital & pooling
