The Core Connecting Idea
IBC 2016 fundamentally shifted India's insolvency landscape from a debtor-friendly, multi-forum, slow system → to a creditor-driven, time-bound, single-window process. The chain: Loan default → NPA → SARFAESI → if unresolved → IBC → CIRP or Pre-Pack → Resolution (company survives) OR Liquidation (assets distributed). Every institution — NCLT, IBBI, CoC, IP — plays a specific non-overlapping role.
⏱️ Time-bound 330 Days 🏦 Creditor-Driven ⚖️ NCLT + IBBI 💡 Pre-Pack for MSMEs 🏆 Swiss Challenge 🛡️ Worker Priority 🏦 Section 227 FSPs
