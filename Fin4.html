The Big Picture Connection
FDI = Stable, long-term, transformational capital (controls assets).   FPI = Market-sensitive, short-term, speculative capital (no control).
Retail FDI rules protect domestic players. E-commerce rules prevent inventory monopolies. SEBI regulates market stability. RBI handles external flows under FEMA 1999. All foreign investment ultimately flows through India's Capital Account of BOP.
🏭 FDI = Control 📊 FPI = No Control 🏪 Retail FDI Rules 📜 FEMA 1999 ⚖️ SEBI + RBI 💰 Capital Account BOP
