🔁 How the 3 Funds Connect
CFI = main vault (tax + loans, Parliament approval). Contingency = emergency bypass (President draws, Parliament restores later). Public Account = trustee money (not govt's — no approval needed). All three flow into and out of the government's financial system.

The Core Connecting Idea — Budget as Democratic Control
Budget is Parliament's supreme instrument of financial control. Tax proposed → Finance Bill (Art.110) → Parliament approves. Spending proposed → Demand for Grants (Lok Sabha only) → Appropriation Bill (Art.114) → CFI withdrawal (Art.266). Emergencies → Contingency Fund (President draws). Trustee money → Public Account (no approval needed). CAG audits all overspending → Reports → PAC scrutinizes. DRSC examines ministry demands before voting. Guillotine enforces time discipline. Rajya Sabha can only discuss — never vote on spending grants.
📜 Art. 112 = Budget ⚖️ Art. 265 No Tax w/o Law 🏦 Art. 266 CFI 💰 Finance Bill 75 Days ⚡ Guillotine = Speaker 🔍 CAG → PAC 🚨 Contingency = President 🤝 Public Account = Trustee
