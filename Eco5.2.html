Pillar 4 — Mandatory

📊 Social Impact Assessment (SIA)

✅ Public Purpose Established
Must justify the acquisition need

Show no viable alternative exists

✅ Minimum Land Required
Only necessary land to be taken

The Core Idea Connecting All Four Sections
India's land challenge has always been the same: how to enable development without destroying livelihoods.
The 2013 Act replaced colonial exploitation with fair compensation and consent. Leasing reform unlocked productivity by formalising hidden tenancy. Land pooling gave an alternative to coercive acquisition. Digitization made property rights transparent and bankable. Together — they form a modern land governance architecture.
