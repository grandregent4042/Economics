The Core Connecting Idea
All three indices measure inflation — but at different points in the supply chain. CPI = what consumers pay. WPI = what producers receive. GDP Deflator = how the entire economy's prices moved. RBI uses CPI because it directly impacts people's cost of living.
🛒 CPI = Retail 🏭 WPI = Wholesale 📐 Deflator = Economy ⭐ RBI → CPI 📅 Both Base = 2011-12
