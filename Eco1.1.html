Depreciation

⚠️ Gross vs Net Investment

Depreciation = Consumption of Fixed Capital (machines wear out)
Net = Gross − Depreciation
₹250 = ₹300 − ₹50
TYPEMEANING
Gross InvestmentTotal capital produced (ignores wear)
Net InvestmentActual increase in productive capacity
DepreciationValue lost = CCA (Capital Consumption Allowance)

The Core Connecting Idea
India built a constitutional framework for local urban democracy in 1992. The real challenge remains: converting legal decentralization into fiscal and administrative reality. Without funds + autonomy, municipalities remain weak despite the law. With genuine empowerment, cities become the engines of India's sustainable development — delivering services, deepening democracy, and driving 60% of GDP from just 3% of land.
🏙️ Legal → Real 💸 Fiscal Federalism 🗳️ Democratic Devolution 📈 Cities = Growth ⚖️ Social Equity 💰 Savings = Investment 📊 3 GDP Methods
