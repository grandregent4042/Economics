IRGD — Debt Sustainability Key

⚖️ Interest Rate vs Growth Rate

IRGD = Interest Rate − Growth Rate
Growth Rate > Interest Rate → ✅ Sustainable
Debt ratio falls automatically over time
Interest Rate > Growth Rate → ❌ Unsustainable
Debt snowballs — requires primary surplus
📌 India: Interest rate historically < growth rate → Debt sustainable

The Big Connected Flow
Fiscal Policy → Deficit → Borrowing → Debt → FRBM imposes discipline.
Debt sustainability depends on IRGD: if growth exceeds interest rate, debt ratio falls automatically. FRBM caps deficit at 3%, debt at 60%/40%, and forces transparency through mandatory statements. When rules are broken (COVID), escape clauses allow limited deviation — but fiscal consolidation must follow.
📊 FRBM = Fiscal Discipline ⚖️ IRGD = Sustainability Test 🏦 RBI ≠ Unlimited Lender 🌐 External Debt = USD Dominated 📋 3F: Deficit → Debt → Sustainability
