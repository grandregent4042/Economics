The Core Concept Flow
Financial markets split into capital market (long-term >1yr) and money market (short-term <1yr). Companies issue securities through primary markets (IPO) and secondary markets (BSE/NSE). People store money as demand deposits (current/savings) or time deposits (FD/RD). NRIs have three options: FCNR (foreign currency, tax-free), NRE (rupee, tax-free, fully repatriable), NRO (rupee, taxable, limited repatriation).
📈 Capital vs Money 🏦 CP ≠ CD Issuer 🔐 Demand vs Time 💱 FCNR = Foreign ❌ NRO = Taxable
