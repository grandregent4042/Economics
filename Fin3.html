⑦ The Full Ecosystem — Everything Connected
📊
The Complete Connection
Government needs money → issues G-Secs via RBI auctions on E-Kuber → Banks buy (satisfy SLR) → CCIL settles via NDS-OM → RBI manages liquidity via Repo/OMO → Investors diversify through MFs, ETFs, AIFs, REITs, ESG → Companies raise global capital via ADR/GDR → Retail investors access via RBI Retail Direct 2021
