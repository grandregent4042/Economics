Two Methods of ARC

🔧 How ARC Works

🏗️
Method 1: Asset Reconstruction
Buys the rights/interest in the loan from the bank. Takes over as new creditor. Pursues recovery.
Rights transfer
📜
Method 2: Securitization
Converts illiquid loans → marketable securities. Sells to investors. Raises liquidity.
Illiquid → Liquid

The Connecting Thread
Developing countries need long-term finance → Private banks hesitate → DFIs created as gap-fillers. Banks lend → some loans go bad → NPAs rise → balance sheets stressed → ARCs buy bad loans. ARCs use two tools: asset reconstruction (takes over rights) or securitization (converts illiquid loans → tradable securities). SARFAESI 2002 is the legal backbone — empowering secured creditors to recover without courts. Financial stability = DFIs channeling credit + ARCs cleaning the system.
🏦 DFI = Gap Filler 💥 NPA = 90+ Days 📊 Stressed = NPA + Restr. + W/off 🏢 ARC = Bad Loan Buyer 📜 Securitize = Illiquid → Liquid
