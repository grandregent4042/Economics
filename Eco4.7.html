Challenges in GST

⚠️ Problems That Remain

🔄 Inverted Duty Structure
Input tax rate > Output tax rate → Refund blockage (e.g. Raw mat. 18%, Product 12%)
🚫 Exclusions from GST
Alcohol, Petroleum, Electricity, Real estate (partial), Land — embedded taxes remain
📊 Threshold Evasion
Turnover <₹40 lakh exempt → Firms split to stay below threshold
💼 Informal Sector Burden
Heavy compliance, digital requirements, filing burden on small businesses
🌐 Multi-Rate Complexity
5 slabs + cess = complex system, classification disputes

The Core Connecting Idea
Fragmented indirect taxes → cascading → GST as One Nation, One Tax, One Market. ITC is the engine that eliminates cascading. GST Council balances federal interests — but SC 2022 confirmed states retain sovereignty. Compensation Cess protected states from revenue shocks. What remains unresolved: exclusions (petroleum, alcohol), inverted duty structure, and informal sector compliance gaps.
