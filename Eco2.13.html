③ Currency Swap — Two Types
Type 1 — Corporate Currency Swap

🏢 Between Two Companies

US Co borrows in US @ 6%
Has natural advantage in home market
Indian Co borrows in India @ 9%
Has natural advantage in home market
They SWAP the principal amounts
Each uses other's cheaper borrowing
End of period → Reverse swap
Principal returned at pre-agreed rate
Purpose: Cheaper foreign borrowing + Hedge exchange rate risk
📌 Definition
Exchange of loan principal in two different currencies at start, swapping back at end at pre-agreed rate → No exchange rate risk.

④ Forex Swap — RBI ↔ Banks (Different from Currency Swap!)
Forex Swap Mechanics

🔄 How Forex Swap Works

Step 1: RBI buys $1B from bank → gives ₹70B
Rupees injected into system
Step 2 (after 3 years): RBI returns $1B
Bank returns ₹70B + Premium
Auction-based: Highest premium wins
Banks compete on premium amount
🔥 March 2019 Example
RBI used forex swap to inject liquidity when OMO limits were exhausted. Out-of-market → avoids direct dollar purchase → prevents rupee depreciation.
Why RBI Uses Forex Swap

💡 Purpose & Mechanism

💧
Inject Rupee Liquidity
Without buying govt bonds
🏦
When OMO Exhausted
Alternative liquidity tool
📉
Prevent Depreciation
Out-of-market transaction
⚙️
Monetary Policy Tool
Manages credit flow
Premium = Extra rupees bank promises to return to RBI. Higher premium → bank wins the auction.
